## Task Prioritization Policy

**Purpose**

This document defines how tasks are prioritized based on impact and urgency.

*Priority Levels*

- P0 (Critical)
System outage
Security issue
Revenue-impacting problem
Needs immediate attention
- P1 (High)
Core feature broken
High customer impact
Should be handled quickly
- P2 (Medium)
Moderate issue or improvement
Normal priority work
- P3 (Low)
Minor issue
Nice-to-have improvement

### How to Decide Priority

1. Impact

How many users are affected?
Is revenue or business blocked?

2. Urgency

Does this need to be fixed immediately?
Is there a deadline?

*Simple Rules*
High impact + high urgency → P0 or P1
High impact + low urgency → P1 or P2
Low impact → P2 or P3
